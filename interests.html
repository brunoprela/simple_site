Interests Things I like

Entrepreneurship

Working for myself has always interested me. In the future I hope to follow in the steps of other great entrepreneurs in influencing the way the world works.



Technology

The development of technology and its impact on human life is a huge part of what I like to study. In particular I love developments in computer vision and biotechnology.



Writing

In addition to writing code, I often squeeze in the time to write fiction and poetry myself. Writing eases stress and is a great way to express oneself. My favorite writer is Jorge Borges.



Philosophy

Reading what people have thought about the human condition and its desire for meaning is always entertaining to me. Some of my favorite philosophers are Nitche, Camus, Kafka and Dostoyevsky.



Psychology

Understanding how the human mind works is something that I've always wanted to be able to do. Psychology has provided an wonderfully amazing and oftentimes counterintutive perspective on this subject.



Friends

These are the people in my life who keep me sane when everything else wants to go to mush.



Photography

I have always loved composing and taking photos. In fact, all of the photos on this page of my site are originals.



Art

Art is a vague term for a part of humanity that I don't think I'll ever understand. Maybe two kids in NYC should have better things to do than go to art museums.



Family

My parents and my little brother (pictured) are the most wonderful things in my life. This list is not in order of importance it seems.



Music

The process of enjoying and analyzing music highlights every other part of life. Kanye West 2020.



Design

The way everything we create is designed is something that I have been studying in every different form of art for the past few years.



Manufacturing

Sometimes it is not enough to just design something. How things are actually produced and enter the market is a huge topic of interest.



Economics

The way the world works is often full of humans, and often full of mistakes as a result. Figuring out how to model these mistakes and perhaps how to see through them and predict them is at the core of economics.



Medicine

Biology, chemistry, technology and their intersection in medicine are of great interest to me. With new developments in medicine, the scale of life can change dramatically; and I hope to be one of the developers pushing it forward. I used to want to go to med school, if you could believe it.



Products

One day I hope to be able to say I have created great software, devices, clothing, etc. that people have enjoyed and which have improved peoples' time on this planet.
